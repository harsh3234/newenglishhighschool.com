About Primary School

The life of a child to be embodied in education starts from the Primary Level. The Student here is introduced to the process of learning. The Primary Level is from 1st to 4th with 16 division managed under the supervision of Mrs. Jyoti Gurnani.

The main objective of the Curriculum at the Primary Level:

  • Emphasis on improving the conceptual understanding of core subjects.
  • Thematic and integrated learning to explore lessons outside the textbook.
  • Introduction to Language Training, Logical Reasoning and Creative Learning.



About High School

The sense of Responsibility, Dedication towards Education, Tolerance, Perseverance, Self Reliability sense of labor efforts and hard work are the training provided in the Secondary Level.

The Secondary Level is from 5th to 10th std with 26 divisions managed under the supervision of Mrs. Jyoti Gurnani. The main objective of the Secondary Curriculum

  • To acquire necessary Knowledge, Skills and Attitudes for Self Development.
  • Develop ability for Inquiry, Critical Thinking and Rational Judgement.
  • To build a foundation for Technological Development, further Education and Training.



About Junior College

The Student in Higher Secondary Level becomes mature and rational. His / Her main intention is to choose a course which is career-oriented.

New English is dignified with three faculties:

Science with 640 seats in XI and 640 in XII with Bifocal subjects CS/Elect/SMR and IT.

Commerce with 1050 seats in XI and 1050 seats in XII.

Arts [Marathi Medium] with 160 seats in XI and 160 seats in XII.

The Main Branch is supervised under the guidance of Mrs. Jyoti Gurnani. The main objective of the curriculum at the Higher Secondary Level is:

  • Inculcation of Wisdom and Knowledge.
  • Development of Leadership Qualities.
  • Creating a Skill-Oriented Personality.


About Night College

The institution established the Kurla Camp Branch in order to motivate the local communities to relish the fruits of education.

The main intention was to break the chain of backwardness.

New English Junior College Kurla Camp Branch Functions for only Commerce faculty with 300 seats in XI and 300 seats in XII with IT.

The Kurla Camp Branch is under the supervision of Mr. Murli Badlani.

The main objective of the branch:

  • To Create a Scope for Education.
  • To Cultivate values of Discipline, Duty, Respect
  • To Groom the Students to Better Prospects.
