About Us

Best college in Ulhasnagar for Science stream

‘The New Education Society’ is a renowned institution that strives hard to provide good education to the students and achieve perfection in each and every field. According to Napoleon Hill “Cherish your vision and your dreams as they are the children of your soul”. Thus the management of ‘The New Education Society’ truly follows their dreams and achieves great things.

Having been established with the sole aim of serving humanity through education ‘New English’ shoulders a major responsibility of catering to the needs of students from various strata of society and works for the moral, physical, intellectual, and personality development of the students entrusted to her care irrespective of caste, color or creed.

‘The New Education Society’ was founded in the year 1959 and got registered with Charity Commissioner, approving the establishment of the school in Ulhasnagar – 421004 in both Sindhi and English Medium.

The society started ‘NEW ENGLISH SCHOOL’ in the year 1959 and Late Shri G.S. Pamnani became the founder Principal of the School. The first batch of SSC with 18 students appeared in Board Examination in the year March 1968 (Sindhi Medium) and in the year March 1972 (English Medium) with 100% results.

From a tiny mustard seed sown 62 years ago NEW ENGLISH has grown and flourished into a mighty majestic multi-dimensional institution giving Ulhasnagar a thorough face-lift.

Establishing an English Medium School in those days was a difficult and challenging task yet having a great sense of social duty, a burning desire to achieve a definite goal in the field of education Shri G.S. Pamnani faced the unfavourable circumstances with great determination. He toiled day and night to develop the school, knocking various doors up to Sachivalaya. Finally his hard work bore fruits.

In just 17 years New English Junior College has reached the strength of 4800 students in Std. XI and XII.
Today college is having maximum number of students learning Information Technology and Bi-Focal Subjects (Computer Science / Electronics / SMR)

The motto of the school “knowledge is power” has always kept the managing committee onward to provide a healthy environment for better enhancement of student’s growth. Keeping in line with our commitment to provide the students with superior support and latest technologies.

We, ‘The New Education Society’ look forward that every student who learns in our institution should shine like a star in the society and we assure to enable pupils to blossom into worthy citizens of our country.
